
Place insurance card images here for processing.

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, WEBP

Examples of what to include:
- Screenshots of digital insurance cards
- Scanned insurance card images  
- Photos of physical insurance cards
- Health insurance cards
- Auto insurance cards
- Any insurance card with text

The processor will extract:
- Policy numbers
- Member IDs
- Carrier information
- Coverage details
- Contact information
- Dates and amounts
